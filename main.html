Informatics and Computer, Politeknik Negeri Ujung Pandang

An Informatics and Computer Science study program equips students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, software development methodologies, database management, web development technologies, computer networks, cybersecurity principles, artificial intelligence, data science techniques, and human-computer interaction. Through a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical experience, students learn to design and implement software solutions, analyze and manage data, secure information systems, develop user-friendly interfaces, and apply emerging technologies like AI and machine learning. The program also emphasizes ethical considerations, legal aspects of technology, and project management methodologies, preparing graduates for diverse career opportunities in software development, IT consulting, data analytics, cybersecurity, and related fields.
